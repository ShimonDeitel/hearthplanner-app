Common questions

Does Hearth sync across devices?

Not yet. Hearth currently stores your family's plan on-device only, which keeps things private but doesn't sync between an iPhone and iPad automatically.

Can I export a progress report?

Yes — from a kid's progress view you can export a summary of completed lessons per subject.

How many kids can I add?

As many as your family needs. There's no limit on the number of kid profiles.

I found a bug. What should I include in my report?

Your iOS version, iPhone model, and the steps that led to the issue. Screenshots help a lot too.
