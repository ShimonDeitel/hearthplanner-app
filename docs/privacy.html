Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Hearth is designed around a simple principle: your family's homeschool records are your business, not ours.

What Hearth collects

Nothing. Hearth does not create an account, does not require sign-in, and does not transmit your kids' names, lessons, subjects, or progress to any server. Everything is stored locally in the app's on-device database.

Children's information

Any kid profiles, lesson records, or notes you add to Hearth are stored only within Hearth's local app storage on your device. This information is never uploaded anywhere and is never shared with any third party.

Analytics and advertising

Hearth contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party trackers of any kind.

Data deletion

Deleting the Hearth app from your device permanently removes all of its data. There is no cloud backup to separately delete.

Children's privacy (COPPA)

Hearth is a tool for parents to plan their children's education and is used by the parent, not directly by children. Hearth does not collect personal information from children because it does not collect any information at all — everything stays on-device.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@shimondeitel.com
